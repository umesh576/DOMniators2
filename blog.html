Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that utilizes statistics, algorithms, and technology to extract insights and knowledge from data. It integrates data analysis, machine learning, and domain expertise to address complex problems and enhance decision-making across various industries

Tech fest

Information Technology Fest, often referred to as Techfest, is an annual event that focuses on science and technology. This festival attracts participants from all over the world, including students, academics, and other members of the general public, and includes competitions, lectures, workshops, and exhibitions to spread science and technology.

Seminar

A seminar is an educational event where a small group of people gather to discuss a specific topic, often led by one or two presenters who guide the conversation. These gatherings are designed to facilitate deeper understanding and discussion rather than just delivering information, and they are typically more interactive than lectures.
